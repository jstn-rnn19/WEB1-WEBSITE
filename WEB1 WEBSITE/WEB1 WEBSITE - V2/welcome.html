How It Works

Step 1

Input Your ingredients First, the user inputs the individual cost for every ingredient and component required to make their final product.

Step 2

Define Your Target Profit (Margin) Next, the user specifies the desired profit margin they need to achieve for the product.

Step 3

Calculate the Selling Price Finally, the system combines the total costs and the desired profit margin to instantly generate the recommended selling price.

About Us
Inspiration

we started it based on real-world observations.

The most painful thing we saw was the stress—the relentless, non-stop worry and calculation they went through, constantly doubting if their pricing was right. This struggle with complicated costing led to underpriced products, financial strain, and ultimately, failure.

They weren't failing because their products weren't amazing; they were failing because they lacked the right tools for costing.

Mission

We created this streamlined calculator to eliminate that stress and stop the cycle of accidental bankruptcy.

Our tool takes your detailed ingredient costs, subtracts all the complexity, and instantly combines it with your target profit margin. The result is the exact selling price you need for guaranteed profitability.
